About Me

I'm a scenic painter who loves to get stuck in. I'm not shy about lifting heavy things or getting my hands painty. I prioritise high quality of work while making efficient use of time and materials to meet project deadlines.

I love learning new techniques and have experience with colour matching, creating various textures with addenden, artex, orbital sander, bonding and grit, distressing and tying in, washes, dry brushing, sea sponge, clarke spray gun, hopper spray gun, textured plastering effects, wood staining, priming/fire retardants, wood filling and repair, minor carpentary and restoration, scaffolding climbing and working at height, mock brickwork and tile effects, event space decoration.

I also have skills in horticulture and seamstress work.
